Rotom Phone

Android-based simulation of a Pokédex interface, featuring a custom UI and mobile systems integration

Description:

This project was a collaboration between me and a fellow developer to turn a real smartphone into a functional "Rotom Phone" from the Pokémon series. Rather than just building a simple app, we worked together to create a complete OS overhaul that changes the entire user experience.

    Key Capabilities:
  • System Wrapper:developed a custom "launcher" that locks the phone into the Rotom environment to maintain immersion. This creates a dedicated experience where only our custom apps and notifications are accessible, effectively turning the phone into a specialized piece of hardware.
  • RotomAI Assistant: A smart companion that uses Computer Vision and AI. You can ask it anything about Pokémon including lore or general knowledge. RotomAI also comes with camera integration. You can point the phone at a plushie or a game sprite, and the AI will identify the Pokémon and give you a full breakdown.
  • Pokédex: A deep-dive encyclopedia for every Pokémon in existence designed to mimic a Pokédex from the games. Beyond just looking up data, we built in tools to compare stats between different Pokémon and manage a list of favorites.
  • Trainer Cards: A personalization app where you can design your own Trainer Card with your badges and team. We integrated QR code sharing, so you can tap an icon, show your code to a friend, and they can instantly scan and save your card to their phone.

Tech used: Java | Kotlin | Android | Gemini API
