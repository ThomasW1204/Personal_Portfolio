More About Me

Passions

I have always been driven to create things that don't just work, but also leave an impression. My technical journey has taken me through countless complex systems from game programming to Agentic AI programming. I am at my best when I am transforming logic into a tangible experience. I don't just want to write meaningless code, I want to build systems that feel alive and responsive.

Dreams and Goals

Ever since I was little my ultimate goal has been to become a Game Developer. To me, games are some of the best things about software. They require a perfect blend of art, logic, and storytelling all of which can impact a player in many ways. Being able to express yourself through coding is one of the best parts of coding to me. I dream of creating an immersive world that players can get lost and find wonder in. Game franchises like BioShock and Fallout do immersion so well that it inspired me to pursue these dreams.
